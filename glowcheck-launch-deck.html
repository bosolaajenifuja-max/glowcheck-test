🚀 Build & Launch Plan

From prototype to App Store — a step-by-step guide to getting GlowCheck live

01 / 12

What We're Building

A beauty product analyzer that helps Black women make informed choices

📱 The App

Scan barcodes, get safety scores (0-100), see hazard analysis, check authenticity

💡 The Value

Transparent ingredient info for Type 3/4 hair care. Empowerment through knowledge.

Target: Black women, 18-45 • Market: $2.5B+

PHASE 3

App Store Preparation

Getting into the stores (Weeks 4-8)

🍎 Apple App Store

• Developer account: £99/year
• Privacy nutrition labels
• App preview screenshots
• 24-48hr review

🤖 Google Play

• Developer account: £25 (one-time)
• Privacy policy URL
• Content rating
• Usually faster
