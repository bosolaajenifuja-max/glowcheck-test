## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Data:** If you create an account, we may collect your name, email, and password
- **User Content:** Product submissions, photos of packaging, reviews, and feedback you submit
- **Communications:** If you contact us, we may collect your email and message

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Data:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Features used, time spent in app, crash logs
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (city/region) — not precise GPS

### Third-Party Data
- We may receive product ingredient data from third-party databases (EWG, FDA, cosmetic ingredient APIs)
- We do not control this third-party data
